Bacon's Dog-Safe Treat Recipes

by Kevin Tan

Dog-Safe "Double Chocolate" Chips

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup carob chips (a safe chocolate alternative for dogs)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted, natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup bacon bits (ensure no added onion, garlic, or excessive salt)
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (or substitute with coconut flour for a grain-free option)
  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the peanut butter and eggs until well combined.
  3. Gradually add in the flour, mixing until a dough forms.
  4. Fold in the carob chips and bacon bits until they're evenly distributed.
  5. On a lined baking sheet, drop teaspoon-sized balls of the dough.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the edges start to turn golden.
  7. Let them cool completely before serving to Bacon.

Dog-Safe "Double Chocolate" Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups carob powder (never use cocoa or chocolate)
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder (make sure it's aluminum-free)
  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, stir together the carob powder, oats, shredded coconut, and baking powder.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the eggs and coconut oil until well combined, then mix in the applesauce.
  4. Gradually combine the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  5. Use a spoon to drop cookie-sized amounts onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Flatten them slightly with the back of the spoon.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes or until the cookies are firm to the touch.
  8. Allow to cool thoroughly before giving to Bacon.

Now Enjoy!

Dog-Safe Pumpkin Banana Treats

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (or coconut flour for a grain-free option)
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup pumpkin puree (ensure it's plain pumpkin, not pie filling)
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 2 tablespoons natural, unsweetened peanut butter
  • 1 large egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour and rolled oats.
  3. In another bowl, mix the pumpkin puree, mashed banana, and peanut butter until smooth.
  4. Beat in the egg to the pumpkin mixture.
  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring to combine.
  6. Roll the dough out on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  7. Use a cookie cutter to cut out shapes or simply cut into squares with a knife.
  8. Place the treats on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes until dry and hard.
  9. Let the treats cool completely before serving to Bacon.

Now Enjoy!

Dog-Safe Sweet Potato Bites

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 250°F (120°C).
  2. Wash and slice sweet potatoes into 1/3-inch slices.
  3. Coat with coconut oil and sprinkle cinnamon.
  4. Place on baking sheet and bake for 2-3 hours, flipping halfway.
  5. Allow to cool before serving.

Now Enjoy!

Dog-Safe Berry Yogurt Freeze

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped strawberries

Instructions

  1. Blend yogurt and berries until smooth.
  2. Pour into ice cube trays or silicone molds.
  3. Freeze for at least 4 hours or until solid.
  4. Serve as a frozen treat for your dog.

Now Enjoy!
